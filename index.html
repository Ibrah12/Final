ABOUT

Background

I am Abraham Onyando Nyakundi, a native of Kisii, Kenya, hailing from the vibrant Nyanza province. Currently residing in Makongeni, Kiambu County, just 44km from Nairobi City, I embody the ethos that relentless dedication and unwavering commitment are the cornerstones of success. Born on December 15th, 1999, I bring a unique blend of cultural richness and global perspective to my work.

EDUCATION

My educational journey began in January 2003, setting the stage for a transformative path. From early childhood development to primary and secondary education, I honed my academic prowess and laid the foundation for a promising future. This journey culminated in my pursuit of higher education, culminating in a degree in Computer and Electronic Systems from Mount Kenya University, Thika, Kiambu County. Immersed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, React, and WordPress development, I solidified my expertise in the digital realm.

CAREER

The decision to pursue a career as a web developer was a natural progression, driven by an innate talent for problem-solving and an unwavering focus on task completion. While I cherish moments spent outdoors with loved ones, my true joy stems from the boundless creativity found in coding and technology. Thriving in environments that demand innovation and flexibility, I constantly push boundaries to deliver optimal results. With a global mindset and a commitment to excellence, I am poised to tackle diverse challenges and exceed client expectations worldwide.
